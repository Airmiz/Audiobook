Chapter One.

On a quiet Thursday evening, Maya stepped onto the porch, took a slow breath, and listened to the rain tapping lightly on the cedar roof. She smiled at the small note in her hand and read it once more: "Tomorrow begins now." The words were simple, but they carried weight, like a promise she had finally decided to keep.

Down the street, a train horn echoed across the valley, then faded into silence. Maya closed her eyes for a moment and said, softly but clearly, "I am ready." When she opened them again, the world looked the same, yet everything felt newly possible.
